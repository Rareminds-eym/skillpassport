┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ HOW TO USE                                                                   │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Step 1: Navigate to /college-admin/reports
  Step 2: Select report type (Attendance, Performance, etc.)
  Step 3: Apply filters (optional)
  Step 4: Click "Export Data" dropdown
  Step 5: Choose format (Excel, CSV, or PDF)
  Step 6: File downloads automatically! ✅
