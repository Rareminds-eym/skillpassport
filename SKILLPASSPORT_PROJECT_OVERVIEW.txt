================================================================================
                         SKILLPASSPORT - PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Platform Name: SkillPassport
Type: Digital Credential & Career Development Platform
Tech Stack: React, TypeScript, Vite, Supabase, Cloudflare Workers, TailwindCSS

================================================================================
                              PLATFORM SUMMARY
================================================================================

SkillPassport is a comprehensive digital credential and career development 
platform that connects students, educational institutions, and recruiters. 
It provides a unified ecosystem for:

  - Students to build digital identity and track learning journey
  - Institutions to manage academic operations
  - Recruiters to discover verified talent

6. RECRUITERS
