# Add-on Subscription User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Feature Add-on Subscription system in SkillPassport.

## How to Purchase

### Step 1: Access the Add-on Marketplace
1. Log in to your SkillPassport account
2. Navigate to **Settings** → **Subscription** → **Add-ons**
3. Or click on any locked feature to see upgrade options

### Step 2: Choose Your Add-on
1. Browse available add-ons by category
2. Click on an add-on to see details and pricing
3. Choose between Monthly or Annual billing
   - Annual billing saves up to 17% compared to monthly

### Step 3: Complete Payment
1. Click "Purchase" or "Upgrade to Pro"
2. Review the order summary
3. Complete payment via Razorpay (UPI, Cards, Net Banking)
4. Your add-on is activated immediately upon successful payment

## Managing Your Subscriptions

### View Active Subscriptions
1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription** → **My Add-ons**
2. See all your active add-ons with expiry dates

### Cancel a Subscription
1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription** → **My Add-ons**
2. Click "Manage" on the add-on you want to cancel
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Your access continues until the end of the billing period

### Toggle Auto-Renewal
1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription** → **My Add-ons**
2. Toggle the "Auto-renew" switch for any add-on
3. If disabled, you'll receive reminder emails before expiry

## Billing & Payments

### Payment Methods
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)

- Wallets

### Invoices
- Invoices are sent to your registered email

- Full refund within 7 days of purchase if unused

- Contact support@rareminds.in for refund requests

## Renewal Reminders

You'll receive email reminders:
- 7 days before expiry
- 3 days before expiry
- 1 day before expiry

If auto-renewal is enabled, your subscription renews automatically.

## Feature Access

### How Feature Gating Works
- Locked features show a "Pro" badge
- Clicking a locked feature shows upgrade options
- Once purchased, features unlock immediately

### Access Sources
Your access to a feature can come from:
1. **Plan** - Included in your subscription plan
2. **Add-on** - Purchased individually
3. **Bundle** - Part of a purchased bundle

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Failed
1. Check your payment method has sufficient funds
2. Try a different payment method
3. Contact your bank if the issue persists
4. Reach out to support with your order ID

### Feature Still Locked After Purchase
1. Refresh the page
2. Log out and log back in
3. Clear browser cache
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### Can't Find My Add-on
1. Check **Settings** → **Subscription** → **My Add-ons**
2. Verify the payment was successful in your email
3. Check your bank statement for the charge

## FAQ

**Q: Can I upgrade from monthly to annual?**
A: Yes, contact support to switch. You'll receive credit for unused days.

**Q: What happens when my subscription expires?**
A: You lose access to premium features but your data is preserved.

**Q: Can I share my add-on with others?**
A: No, add-ons are tied to individual accounts.

**Q: Do add-ons work on mobile?**
A: Yes, all features work on mobile browsers.

**Q: Can I get a trial before purchasing?**
A: Some features offer limited free usage. Check individual add-on pages.
