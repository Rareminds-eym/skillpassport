# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Skill Ecosystem?

Skill Ecosystem is a comprehensive educational management and career development platform that connects students, educators, and employers. It provides tools for academic management, AI-powered career guidance, course enrollment, and recruitment pipeline management.

### Who can use Skill Ecosystem?

- **Students:** K-12 through post-graduation
- **Educators:** Teachers and faculty members
- **School/College Admins:** Institutional administrators
- **Recruiters:** HR professionals and hiring managers
- **Parents:** Monitor student progress (coming soon)

### Is Skill Ecosystem free?

We offer different pricing tiers:
- **Basic:** ₹500/student/year - Core academic features
- **Professional:** ₹1000/student/year - Includes career services and analytics
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing - Full features with dedicated support

Individual students can create free accounts to access career assessments and job opportunities.

## For Students

### How do I take a career assessment?

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to "Assessments" in the sidebar
3. Select "Career Interest Assessment"
4. Complete the 60-question RIASEC assessment (15 minutes)
5. View your personalized career recommendations

### What is the RIASEC assessment?

RIASEC is a scientifically-validated career assessment that measures six personality types:
- **R**ealistic (hands-on, practical)
- **I**nvestigative (analytical, research-oriented)
- **A**rtistic (creative, expressive)
- **S**ocial (helping, teaching)
- **E**nterprising (leadership, business)
- **C**onventional (organized, detail-oriented)

Your results help identify careers that match your interests and strengths.

### How accurate are the job recommendations?

Our AI-powered matching system has 85%+ accuracy based on student feedback. It considers:
- Your skills and qualifications
- Career assessment results
- Academic performance
- Interests and preferences
- Job requirements and company culture

### Can I apply to multiple opportunities?

Yes! You can apply to unlimited jobs, internships, and training programs. Your profile and resume are stored, making applications quick and easy.

### How do I track my applications?

Go to "My Applications" to see:
- All submitted applications
- Current status (Applied, Screening, Interview, Offer)
- Timeline of updates
- Next steps and action items

### Are the courses free?

We offer both free and paid courses:
- **Internal courses:** Provided by your institution (usually free)
- **External courses:** Marketplace courses (prices vary)
- **Certifications:** Some require payment for certification

### How do I get a certificate?

Complete all course modules and pass the final assessment. Certificates are automatically generated and available in your "Achievements" section.

## For Recruiters

### How do I post a job?

1. Log in to recruiter dashboard
2. Click "Post Opportunity"
3. Fill in job details (title, description, requirements)
4. Set eligibility criteria (grade level, skills)
5. Publish - AI matching begins automatically

### How does candidate matching work?

Our AI analyzes:
- **Skills:** Required vs. candidate skills
- **Education:** Degree and program match
- **Experience:** Relevant projects and internships
- **Interests:** Career assessment alignment
- **Performance:** Academic grades and achievements

Each candidate receives a match score (0-100).

### Can I search for candidates?

Yes! Use advanced filters:
- Skills and technologies
- Education level and program
- Location preferences
- Availability
- Experience level
- Assessment scores

### How do I manage the hiring pipeline?

1. Applied
2. Screening
3. Interview
4. Offer
5. Hired / Rejected

Add notes, schedule interviews, and communicate directly with candidates.

### Can I export candidate data?

Yes, export to:
- Excel spreadsheet
- CSV file
- PDF reports
- Integration with your ATS

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password

1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

### I'm not receiving emails

Check:
- Spam/junk folder
- Email address is correct in profile
- Email notifications enabled in settings
- Contact support if issue persists

### My assessment results aren't showing

- Ensure you clicked "Submit" (not just "Save")
- Results typically appear within 30 seconds
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if still not visible

### Course videos won't play

- Check internet connection
- Try different browser
- Disable browser extensions
- Clear cache and cookies
- Ensure browser is up to date

### I can't upload documents

- Check file size (max 10MB)
- Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Try different browser
- Contact support for larger files
